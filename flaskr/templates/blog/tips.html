Top Tips for a Better Workout

  • Stay Hydrated: Drink water before, during, and after your workout.
  • Warm Up: Always start with a warm-up to prepare your body.
  • Mix It Up: Incorporate a variety of exercises to keep your routine interesting.
  • Focus on Form: Proper technique reduces the risk of injury.
  • Set Realistic Goals: Establish achievable goals to maintain motivation.
  • Rest and Recover: Allow your body time to heal and regain strength.
